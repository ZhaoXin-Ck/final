About Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced sport played between two teams. Each team aims to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's basket. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.

Rules of the Game

Basketball has various rules governing dribbling, shooting, fouls, and more. Players must follow these rules to ensure fair play and maintain the integrity of the game.
